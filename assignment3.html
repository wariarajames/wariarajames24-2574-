Mental Health Awareness

Mental health awareness helps people understand emotional well-being and how to support each other during stressful times.

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Talking about emotions and seeking help when necessary can improve overall quality of life.

Healthy routines like sleep, exercise, and relaxation can greatly improve mental balance and emotional resilience.

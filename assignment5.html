Mental Health Awareness

Self Care

Taking care of your mental health includes rest, exercise and healthy habits.

Mindfulness

Practicing mindfulness can reduce stress and help people stay present.

Community Support

Talking to friends, family or professionals improves mental wellbeing.

Healthy Habits

  • Sleep regularly
  • Exercise daily
  • Eat healthy foods
  • Practice meditation
    • Breathing exercises
    • Guided meditation
